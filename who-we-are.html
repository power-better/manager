Who We Are/Our Story

Founded in Seattle, USA, Power Better was established by a visionary team of experts in power systems and instrumentation & control engineering. As new energy sources and emerging technologies continue to evolve, energy systems and industrial users face both unprecedented opportunities and increasingly complex challenges.

Traditional consulting firms often struggle to respond effectively to the frequent disruptions that define the sector, while highly skilled independent consultants—though invaluable—remain too scarce to address the full scope of these demands.

Recognizing this gap, Power Better has redefined the role of technical services. By uniting seasoned industry practitioners with forward-thinking innovators, we drive meaningful innovation, solve challenges that others find insurmountable, and deliver reliable solutions that reshape the industry.

Power Better is more than a technology firm—we’re a trusted partner in building a smarter, more efficient future for the power and instrumentation & control sector.

Who We Are/Our Vision
