Who We Are

Established in 2024 in Seattle, our company was formed by a visionary team of experts in power and instrumentation & control engineering. The team members graduated from internationally renowned universities with engineering degrees and possess extensive experience in consulting and engineering within the power industry.

We specialize in providing professional services to the electric power sector, including:

· Power system simulation

· System optimization

· Energy-saving solutions

· Safety evaluations

For industrial clients, we also offer on-site technical support such as:

· Routine maintenance

· Fault diagnosis

· Troubleshooting of electrical and automation equipment

· Provide electrical devices and components
